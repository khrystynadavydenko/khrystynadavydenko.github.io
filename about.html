ABOUT

LIUDMILA

DAVYDENKO

20+

YEARS WORKING IN ART

LIUDMILA DAVYDENKO IS UKRAINIAN ARTIST.

She works in the direction of abstract associative painting and sculpture.

Participant in numerous group exhibitions, winner of awards and prizes as a painter and sculptor. Among them The Honoré de Balzac Award of the "Lviv Alliance Francaise" and first honors degree in the art project "Abstract Art-2019" (Ivano-Frankivsk).Personal exhibitions in Ukraine and abroad are constantly organized.
The works were exhibited at Kyiv Contemporary Art, ART ZURICH, Art Paris, RHY Art Fire Basel, thus are part of private collections in Ukraine, Poland, Russia, Germany, USA, France, Spain and Switzerland.
